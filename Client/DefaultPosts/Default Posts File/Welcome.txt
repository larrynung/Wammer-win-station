非常感謝您嘗試使用我們的App，我叫做 Will，是Waveface的創辦人兼CEO。 
經過半年來的努力，我們從兩人，三人，到現在有二十名熱血的團隊成員，日夜揮灑手指頭上的汗水，把Waveface用程式碼，一步一腳印地生了出來。 

我們自己相信，在這個多元的數位時代，每個人生活的片段都零碎地存在周遭的手機、筆電、桌機或平板電腦裡面。 
而我們自己遭遇的最大的問題，即是各個不同的品牌，都積極地建立屬於自己的私人島嶼，要是您今天有一支Android手機，但是用Windows桌機，iPad平板，光一張用手機照的相片，想要傳到iPad上看，想要儲存到桌機裡，就已經是個大工程。(目前最好的方法還是用Gmail寄信給自己，或是用雲端服務傳送。) 

因此，我產生把大家的數位裝置，搭一座橋樑連結起來的想法，把一個個的私人島嶼打通。 
今天我很高興得把這座橋梁給您使用，希望Waveface會成為您生活中，連結數位記憶的最好幫手。 
請盡情享用！ 

Will, 以及Waveface 團隊敬上。

Hi there!  

Thanks for giving our App a try, I'm Will, founder and CEO of Waveface. And this is a little bit of our story. 
After six months, we started from 3 nerdy founders to a 20 man team. All of the guys and gals in our team busted our big behinds and drained through countless espressos to build Waveface one code at a time.  

We all believe that in this digital era, all our life memories are scattered around multiple gadgets, smart phones, Laptops, desktops, tablets, etc.  

And the biggest problem we encountered is our gadgets are isolated in different islands, created by big brands in the industry. Just imagine taking a photo with your android cell, wanting to view it on your iPad, and back it up on your Windows desktop. The simplest way to do this is sending a mail to yourself via Gmail, or using other cloud services. 

Based on this problem, I came up with the thought of building a bridge between all these islands, blocked by invisible digital oceans, and give everyone a simple, comfortable, and stylish way to connect your digital life.  

Today, I'm proud to say we constructed that bridge, and we hope Waveface can be a part of your life.  
Enjoy! 

Will & Team Waveface
